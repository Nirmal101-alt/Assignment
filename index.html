Welcome to my place Mushalpur which is situated under Baksa District of Assam. A place with beautiful tourism places and beautiful kind hearted people. Mushalpur is a small town the headquarters of Baksa district in Assam state of India.. Demographics. Mushalpur has population of 795 of which 429 are males while 366 are females as per 2011 census of India.In Mushalpur population of children with age 0-6 is 69 which makes up 8.68% of total population of the town.
